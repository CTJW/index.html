Psychology

What Do We Know?

What is psychology?

Psychology can be broken down into different catergories. You have cognitive which focuses on the brain. There is social psychology which focuses on the social aspect, but for the sake of this being a homework assignment for a coding bootcamp, let's just say it is the study of human behavior.

How do we use psychology?

Psychology is used on an everyday basis. Some use it knowningly and others are not aware that they are using it. Examples include:
  • A psychologist treating a patient
  • Subliminal messages
  • Manipulation
Each of these examples are a form of psychology.

Who is psychology for?

Psychology is for anyone. It is here to be a helpful tool to help those that need it maneuver through life's difficulties with resilience.
Children that suffer with behavioral issues will typically receive guidance from a therapist who specializes in psychological techniques.

Teens that struggle with peer pressure, bullying or abuse benefit from psychology.

Adults who need a little help or someone to talk with inorder to help them function better in society also benefit from psychology.

And there are always those who are just curious that want to learn and help others.
